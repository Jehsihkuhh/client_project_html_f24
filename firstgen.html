A Guide for First Gen Wolverines

Welcome First Generation Master's Students!

Welcome to the University of Michigan! Now that you've made it here, we hope you stay! As a first-generation Master's student, you're embarking on a transformative journey. You may face unique challenges and daunting experiences, but do not fret, you are not alone. There are many resources the University has to help navigate your time in grad school successfully.

Academic Success

There are many resources for first-gen students to ensure academic success.

  • Ask a Librarian: Have library or research-related question? Ask a librarian is a free resource for students to ask staff questions and schedule consultation appointments.
  • Instructional Support Services: For LSA faculty and students, Instructional Support Services provide learning envrionments inside and outside the classroom.
  • Mentoring & Advising: Participate in a variety of mentorship and adivising programs like, Mentoring Others Results in Excellence (MORE), or The ADVANCE program.

Join First-Gen for Weekly Events!

University of Michigan holds many weekly events for ALL first-gen students meant to connect with peers, gain valuable insights, and build a supportive community. These events will take place at a variety of times and in every corner of the campus so be sure to stay up to date with the calendar so you don't miss out!
