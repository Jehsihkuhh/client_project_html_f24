Center For The Education of Women

Who is CEW+?

The Center for the Education of Women (CEW+) at the University of Michigan is a pioneering organizational hub dedicated to advancing the academic, professional, and personal aspirations of women and historically marginalized individuals within the university community. Established with a commitment to fostering equity, diversity, and inclusion, CEW+ offers a comprehensive range of support services, including individual counseling, financial aid through scholarships, and an array of educational workshops and events. As a beacon of empowerment and advocacy, CEW+ strives to create a more inclusive environment that enables all voices to be heard and valued.

What We Do?

  • Events + Workshop

    CEW+ at the University of Michigan hosts a dynamic range of events and workshops designed to empower and support women and historically marginalized individuals throughout their academic and professional journeys. These offerings include professional development sessions on resume building, interview skills, and leadership training, as well as academic support workshops that cover writing techniques, research skills, and time management. Additionally, CEW+ addresses personal growth and wellness through sessions on work-life balance, stress management, and financial literacy.   • Counseling

    We provide career counseling and education counseling. Our comprehensive counseling program is tailored to support the unique needs of women and other historically marginalized populations within the university community. The center offers both individual and group counseling sessions, designed to assist clients with career and educational planning, work-life balance, and overcoming personal and professional obstacles. If interested, there are three ways to schedule an appointment:

    1. Fill out this online form and a member of our team will contact you via phone or email (your preference), to schedule your appointment within one business day.
    2. Call (734) 764-6360 between the hours of 8:30 am and 4:30 pm Monday - Friday. If we are unavailable, please leave us a message and we will return your call as quickly as possible.
    3. Email us at cew.appts@umich.edu and we will get back to you as quickly as possible. Please be advised, if there is a high-volume of emails, you may not get the first available appointment.
